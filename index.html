Mission & Vision
Structured, inclusive and circular waste systems.
Mission
To ensure waste generated in the
economy reaches its nal destination
through a structured, inclusive, and
circular system.
Vision
To transform the informal waste value
chain in India by creating structured
storage & segregation, empowering
informal workers, generating green
jobs and moving toward 100%
recyclability.

How It Works
Simple 4 step ow
1
Segregate your waste using Grabbish bins
Use the color-coded bins and our in-app segregation tutor to sort wet, dry and hazardous waste.
2
Schedule a pickup via app or chatbot
Pick a slot or ask the bot to book recurring collections.
3
Hand over waste at your doorstep
Our trained collection partner will take it bags or bins ready.
4
Track impact & earn rewards
Watch your recycled kg, CO saved and redeem points for rewards.
